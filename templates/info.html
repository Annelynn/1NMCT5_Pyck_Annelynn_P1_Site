Why

This project was created as an assignment for school. Students of New Media and Communication Technology are supposed to create a project to combine all the stuff they have learned in the past year. ShelfChecker is such a project. It combines the newly acquired knowledge in devices, users, connections and data. These four things are at the base of this undergraduate programme.
